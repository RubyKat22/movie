These are biggest Flims that has impacted our soctiy over the years

Titanic: the tragic love story between lovers trying to fight to survive the tragic event of the Titanic sinking. From the iconic scenes in this movie all the way to its beautiful stories.


Casablanca: during the age of great sadness where there was hate during World War 2 shows that Love can still happen in the darkest moments. Sacrificing oneself for the love of another.


Jaws: a simple story of man and animal fighting for survival in this big world. With innocent people dying to the hand of a shark giving a man purpose.

Jurassic Park: Man trying to have the power to be God backfires and learns to not play with life and that Life finds a way to be more powerful.


Avatar: Humans trying to control another world and destroy it when they find out the beauty it is within. Soon fights back to stop the distortion of their world.


The Wizard of Oz: one our first colorful films shows the story of another world and to escape and be better than ourselves. To learn that what we wanted was in ourselves the whole time.
